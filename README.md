
## **HR Analytics & Employee Attrition Intelligence Dashboard**

---


## Project Overview

The **HR Analytics & Employee Attrition Intelligence Dashboard** is an interactive data visualization solution designed to analyze workforce metrics, employee attrition patterns, demographic distribution, and job satisfaction levels.

The objective of this project was to transform raw HR data into actionable insights that support data-driven decision-making in workforce planning, retention strategy, and talent management.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of employee distribution, attrition trends, department performance, education background, age groups, and job satisfaction metrics.

---

## Key Workforce Metrics

* **Total Employees:** 1,470
* **Attrition Count:** 237
* **Attrition Rate:** 16.12%
* **Active Employees:** 1,233
* **Average Employee Age:** 37 years

These KPIs provide leadership with a high-level understanding of workforce stability and organizational health.

---

## Key Insights & Analysis

### 1️⃣ Department-wise Attrition

* Sales department shows the highest attrition contribution (~56%).
* R&D contributes ~38% of attrition.
* HR has the lowest attrition (~5%).

**Business Insight:** Sales roles may require targeted retention programs, compensation review, or workload balancing strategies.

---

### 2️⃣ Age Group Distribution

* Highest employee concentration is in the **25–34 age group**.
* Attrition is significant in the early-career segment (Under 34).
* Workforce gradually declines in higher age brackets.

**Business Insight:** Early-career employees may need engagement programs, career growth planning, and mentorship initiatives.

---

### 3️⃣ Gender-based Attrition Analysis

* Male attrition is higher compared to female attrition.
* Certain age groups (25–34) show higher attrition rates across genders.

**Business Insight:** Helps HR design gender-inclusive retention strategies and engagement policies.

---

### 4️⃣ Job Satisfaction Analysis

* Job satisfaction levels vary significantly across roles.
* Sales Executive and Research Scientist roles show high employee counts but varying satisfaction levels.
* Lower satisfaction ratings in specific roles may correlate with higher attrition.

**Business Insight:** Identifies departments requiring managerial intervention and work environment improvements.

---

### 5️⃣ Education Field-wise Attrition

* Employees from Life Sciences and Medical backgrounds show higher attrition counts.
* Technical and Marketing fields contribute moderately.

**Business Insight:** Recruitment and retention strategies can be aligned with education-specific trends.
